Green Day’s “She” like you’ve never heard it before

I recently discovered this Canadian Pop-Punk Cover Band on YouTube called Minority 905. They do a lot of different covers of mainly Pop-Punk bands and I must say that they are one of best cover bands I have heard in awhile. They made an amazing cover of one of my top 10 favorite Green Day songs, she, and is definitely worth a listen as well as New Found Glory’s Hit or Miss. They also do a lot of their own content. I recommend “Shadow”.

Feb. 8th 2017

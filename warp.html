Are you ready for Warped Tour?

Warped Tour is a huge event with dozens of bands and artists many of which you will never have heard of. It’s a great way to discover new bands or meet bands you love for a pretty cheap price. Warped Tour is great and all but unless you have a plan you aren’t going to make the best of your experience.

  • 1. Make sure you get a map of Warped Tour and scheduled times
    There’s going to be times where two bands you like are playing at the same time and you wanna make sure that you choose the best one.
  • 2. Bring one closed plastic water bottle as well as an empty large water bottle
    At Warped Tour you are allowed to bring only one water bottle in and it must be sealed and trust me you don’t want to be paying 4$ for more. That’s why you bring a larger empty bottle for the refill stations around Warped Tour, although these lines are going to be very long at least you aren’t spending all you money on things that could go towards some band merch.
  • 3. Go with friends
    Going with friends makes the experience so much better although be sure to bring friends that have similar music taste to you because you don’t wanna be stuck at a band you hate while missing one of you favorite bands. (Happened to me don’t let it happen to you)

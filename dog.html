Jake Ewald from Modern Baseball’s New Music

Jake has been working on a side project away from Modern Baseball called Slaughter Beach, Dog and it brings a whole new vibe to the already classic voice Modern Baseball is known for. There are a lot of similarities as to Modern Baseball although it’s more slower as well as laid back. There’s more of a “chill” vibe to Slaughter Beach, Dog, where Modern Baseball has more of a “edgy” sound.
